ishare example


ishare基本参数说明
*mod:模式(0->图标式,1->悬浮右侧,2->悬浮左侧)
*icon:图标大小(0->小,1->中,2->大)
*share:分享到哪些社交站点,采用数组形式,数组可增删，图标符号按数组顺序出现，最少必须保留一项(tsina->新浪微博,tqq->腾讯微博,QQ->QQ,qzone->QQ空间,qqmail->qq邮箱,kaixin001->开心网,renren->人人网,weixin->微信朋友圈,fb->facebook,twitter->twitter)
*top:悬浮式必须，菜单距离浏览器顶部距离如150px
*bgStyle:悬浮式必须,菜单配色，目前提供8种颜色(blue,black,gray,blues,green,org,red,pink)
*isShowTime:是否显示分享次数，无此参数则默认不显示
*shareword:指定分享内容，无此参数则默认抓取title内容
*shareurl:指定分享的url地址,无此参数默认分享页面url
*sharepic:指定分享的图片地址，无此参数则默认抓取本页宽高大于150px的第一张图
*ContentId:ishare出现的位置，无此参数则代码在什么位置ishare就显示在此处
红色为必选项

1.基本用法:



  <script>
	iShareNew({
			"mod":0,
			"icon":2,
			"share":["tsina","tqq","QQ","qzone","mail","kaixin001","renren","fb","twitter","weixin"]
	});
 </script>

2.指定分享内容&显示分享次数:



  <script>
	iShareNew({
				"mod":0,
				"icon":2,
				"share":["tsina","QQ","tqq","qzone"],
				"shareword":"ishare腾讯网UED出品社会化分享插件",
				"isShowTime":true,
				"shareurl":"http://www.qq.com/",
                                "sharepic":"http://mat1.gtimg.com/joke/tomiezhang/ishare/t.png"
			});
 </script>

3.悬浮式右侧&修改icon次序:



  <script>
	iShareNew({
				"mod":1,
				"icon":1,
				"top":"150px",
				"bgStyle":"green",
				"share":["kaixin001","QQ","qzone","tsina","tqq","mail","renren","fb"],
				"shareword":"ishare腾讯网UED出品社会化分享插件",
				"isShowTime":true,
				"shareurl":"http://www.qq.com/",
                 "sharepic":"http://mat1.gtimg.com/joke/tomiezhang/ishare/t.png"
			});
 </script>

4.将图标按钮显示到指定容器:



  <div id="ishareWraps" style="width:80%;border:3px dashed #ccc;margin:20px auto;padding:20px;"></div>
  <script>
	iShareNew({
				"mod":0,
				"icon":2,
				"share":["tsina","tqq","QQ","qzone","mail","kaixin001","renren","fb","twitter","weixin"],
				"shareword":"ishare腾讯网UED出品社会化分享插件",
				"isShowTime":true,
				"shareurl":"http://www.qq.com/",
                 "sharepic":"http://mat1.gtimg.com/joke/tomiezhang/ishare/t.png",
				"ContentId":"ishareWraps"
			});
 </script>
